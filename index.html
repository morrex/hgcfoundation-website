The Dual Meaning of HGC

Our name embodies a dual philosophical system that constitutes the spiritual core of the foundation:

Dimension 1: Action Guide
H Hope: Lighting a lighthouse in despair.
G Guardian: Enduring security barrier.
C Connection: Breaking barriers of loneliness.
Dimension 2: Growth Path
H Health: Physical and mental rehabilitation.
G Growth: Education rights and skills.
C Care: Inclusive social environment.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To build a "Zero Barriers, Non-Discriminatory, Dignified" inclusive society, where every autistic child can discover their potential and enjoy a colorful childhood.

Mission

  • Empower: Gather funds & tech for high-quality rehab resources.
  • Bridge: Connect families, government, hospitals, and schools.
  • Advocate: Eliminate prejudice and promote policy improvement.

Values

Respect Respect neurodiversity as unique, not a burden.
Scientific Rely on evidence-based medicine; refuse blind aid.
Transparency Uphold financial transparency; honor every kindness.

HGC Core Projects

Building a "Full Life Cycle" Support System

H - Health

Starlight Rehab Support Plan

Focus on physical & mental rehabilitation

  • "Star Wish" Grants: Subsidies for speech, OT, PT therapies.
  • Green Channel: Fast-track diagnosis in professional hospitals.
  • Respite Service: Volunteer care to give parents a break.

G - Growth

Morning Star Inclusive Education

Supporting education rights & skills

  • Art Healing Workshops: Painting, music, drama to unlock potential.
  • "Little Guardians" Camp: Inclusive outdoor activities for friendship.
  • Gardener Award: Training for special education teachers.

C - Connection

Blue Ribbon Advocacy Plan

Creating an inclusive social soil

  • "Light It Up Blue": World Autism Day landmark campaigns.
  • Digital Education: TikTok "100 Questions" series.
  • Policy Seminars: Promoting policy support with universities.
